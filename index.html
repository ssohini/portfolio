About Me

Bridging software, hardware, and decentralization.

I am a passionate developer working at the intersection of Blockchain, AI/ML, and hardware-based systems. Currently a student at the Institute of Engineering and Management, Kolkata (IEM), my journey is driven by a deep curiosity to solve tangible, real-world problems through interdisciplinary innovation.

During my academic journey, I have had the privilege to complete in-person paid internships at prestigious institutions like IIT Kharagpur and IIT Madras. Furthermore, my research and practical applications have received validation through project funding from IIT Kharagpur and IEM (after securing 1st place in their idea presentation competition).

I thrive on building complete systems—connecting embedded sensors, smart contracts, and intelligence to build reliable applications. Consistency, problem-solving, and a focus on impactful innovation form the core of my workflow.
